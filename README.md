# Helping SREs succeed with AI.

DevRel & AI Labs Lead at [Rootly](https://rootly.com), building open-source tools and research to advance reliability engineering in the AI era. Former Senior SRE at LinkedIn, where I co-designed a [patented self-healing infrastructure](https://patents.google.com/patent/US9674031B2). Co-founded [Holberton School](https://www.holbertonschool.com), training thousands of software engineers across 25+ countries. Founded [while42](https://while42.org), a developer community of 3,000+ engineers across 40 cities.

I write for [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/author/sylvain-kalache/), [The New Stack](https://thenewstack.io/author/sylvain-kalache/), and [VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/author/sylvain-kalache/), speak at conferences like SREcon, and help SRE and platform teams navigate the AI ecosystem.
